5. WHY AWS SES + CAMPAIGNPILOT IS CHEAPER
================================================================================

A) YOU PAY ONLY FOR WHAT YOU SEND
   - No monthly subscription, no contact limits
   - $0.10 per 1,000 emails sent. That's it.
   - Brevo/Mailchimp charge based on contacts + plan tiers

B) NO CONTACT-BASED PRICING
   - Mailchimp charges based on how many contacts you HAVE
   - Even unsubscribed contacts count toward your billing!
   - AWS SES doesn't care how many contacts you have

C) SENDING LIMITS ARE MASSIVE
   - AWS SES Production: 50,000 emails/day (14 emails/second)
   - Can request increase to UNLIMITED
   - Brevo Free: only 300/day
   - Mailchimp: capped by plan (120k-150k/month)

D) NO FEATURE GATES
   - Brevo charges $9/month just to remove their logo
   - Mailchimp locks automation behind Standard ($20+) plan
   - CampaignPilot: all features included, you own the tool

9. AWS SES SETUP REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

What you need:
- AWS Account (free to create)
- A domain you own (for SPF/DKIM/DMARC verification)
- Production access approval (~24 hours)
- CampaignPilot integration (handled by the tool)

What AWS requires for production access:
- Describe your email use case
- Explain your opt-in process (how users subscribe)
- Show you handle bounces and complaints
- Typical approval time: 24-48 hours
