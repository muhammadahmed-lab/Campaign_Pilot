Why AWS SES?

Right now we're limited to 2,000 emails/day through Gmail SMTP.
AWS SES removes that limit — 50,000 emails/day, scalable to unlimited.

Cost: $0.10 per 1,000 emails.
10,000 emails = $1. That's it. No subscriptions, no contact fees.

Competitors like Mailchimp ($350/mo) and Brevo ($499/mo) charge
10-15x more for the same volume. They also lock you into monthly
plans based on contact count — even unsubscribed contacts cost you.

Other providers like SendGrid ($20/mo for 50K) and Postmark ($15/mo
for 10K) are similar to SES in pricing, but they add platform fees
on top. SES is pure pay-per-send with no base cost.

What makes us different: CampaignPilot (Mera Tool Basically). We own it.
Mailchimp/Brevo are rented platforms — we pay monthly, they control
the pricing, and our data sits on their servers. With CampaignPilot +
SES, we control everything: the tool, the data, the sending, and the
cost.
